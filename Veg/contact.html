Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our products and services.

What types of agricultural products do you specialize in?

We specialize in a wide range of agricultural products including fresh fruits and vegetables, organic produce, grains and pulses, oilseeds, and spices. Our focus is on premium quality products sourced directly from trusted farmers across India.

Do you offer international shipping?

Yes, we have extensive experience in exporting agricultural products worldwide. We handle all necessary documentation, certifications, and logistics to ensure smooth international shipments while maintaining product quality throughout the journey.

What are your minimum order quantities?

Minimum order quantities vary by product. For bulk orders, we typically require a minimum of 1 metric ton, but we also accommodate smaller orders for certain products. Contact our sales team for specific MOQ information based on your needs.

How do you ensure product quality?

We implement strict quality control measures at every stage - from farm selection to packaging and transportation. Our team conducts regular inspections, and we work only with certified farmers who follow best agricultural practices. We also provide quality certifications upon request.
