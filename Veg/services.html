Comprehensive Agro Trading Solutions

At Amanveer Trading Company, we offer end-to-end services to facilitate seamless agricultural trade between India and global markets. Our expertise spans procurement, quality control, packaging, logistics, and compliance to ensure your agricultural sourcing needs are met efficiently and reliably.

Wholesale Trading

We cater to large-scale buyers in India and abroad, providing a consistent supply of agricultural products in bulk quantities. Our team manages procurement, quality inspection, and logistics to ensure timely delivery and competitive pricing.

  • Direct sourcing from trusted farms across India
  • Bulk quantities with flexible order sizes
  • Competitive pricing with volume discounts
  • Reliable supply chain management

Global Export

Amanveer's export services cover everything from shipping and customs documentation to compliance with international standards. We work with trusted freight partners and monitor every stage of the shipping process to guarantee the safe arrival of your goods.

  • End-to-end export documentation and compliance
  • FSSAI, APEDA, and global certification support
  • Sea and air freight options
  • Real-time shipment tracking

Custom Packaging & Private Labeling

We offer customized packaging solutions tailored to your market and branding needs. From designing your labels to selecting the right materials (jute, kraft paper, polypropylene, or vacuum-sealed pouches), we can accommodate both functional and aesthetic requirements.

  • Custom packaging designs for retail and wholesale
  • Private labeling to build your brand identity
  • Material options for different product types
  • Bulk packaging for cost efficiency

Logistics & Supply Chain Management

Our logistics services streamline the entire supply chain—managing procurement, storage, transport, and delivery. We leverage modern inventory management systems to track orders, minimize delays, and provide real-time updates.

  • Temperature-controlled storage facilities
  • Domestic and international transportation
  • Order tracking and inventory management
  • Just-in-time delivery options

Quality Assurance & Compliance

Quality is non-negotiable at Amanveer. Every batch of produce undergoes rigorous inspection and testing to ensure conformity to food safety standards and client specifications. We maintain detailed records of product origin, processing, and testing to provide full traceability.

  • Multi-stage quality control checks
  • Compliance with FSSAI, APEDA, and international standards
  • Product testing and certification support
  • Full supply chain traceability
